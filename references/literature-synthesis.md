# Literature Synthesis

Use this when the task is to synthesize papers, notes, and competing research directions before writing or experimenting.

## Prompt Skeleton

```text
Task type: literature-synthesis
Deliverable: synthesis note or research brief

Research question:
<what the synthesis should answer>

Materials map:
- <path>: paper summary or extracted text
- <path>: reading notes
- <path>: local implementation or experiment note

Question:
Synthesize the key positions, assumptions, methods, limitations, and open questions across these materials.

Required output:
- Shared themes
- Key disagreements
- Method or theory comparison
- Research gaps
- What looks most promising next
- What remains unsupported by the current materials
```
